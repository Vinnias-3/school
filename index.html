Our Story

Crafting Excellence Since 2012

Founded in Stockholm, Henric Studio has grown from a boutique design firm to an internationally recognized creative agency. Our journey has been marked by relentless pursuit of aesthetic perfection and meaningful brand narratives.

We believe in the power of thoughtful design to elevate brands and create lasting impressions. Our approach combines Scandinavian minimalism with global perspectives to deliver timeless solutions.
